

The photo shows 6 individual retail packs. Not all of the sellers are offering this. Carefully read the lower priced sellers' product descriptions.  Some say "not in retail pack" or "sealed in shrink wrap"  These would be OEM Epson cartridges, but are in vacuum packs of 3. These were made to be included with a new printer, where all the cartridges would be inserted at one time.This is the result of the ancient Chinese custom of making "extra" product (whether handbags, sneakers, or printer cartridges) at the end of a production run for a name brand, then selling  on the black market.
