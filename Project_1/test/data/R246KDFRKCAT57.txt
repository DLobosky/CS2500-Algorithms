

Amy Grant's music contains many great Christian pop hits.  Her voice is superb.  Many of her songs are classics.This collection contains many of her all time best including such gems as these:  Stay For Awhile, Everywhere I Go, Angels, Thy Word, Sing Your Praise To The Lord, In A Little While, El Shaddai, and Father's Eyes.No contemporary Christian music collection would be complete without an Amy Grant collection such as this.  I recommend it.
