

While this book does have the f word in it several times, I still give it 5 stars.  Once I started reading it, I had a hard time putting it down.I read the whole thing in one day.  I had to see what would happen on the next page.  I tried to figure out what was going to happen and who was the Leader, but I was wrong.If you like romance and mystery, you are going to enjoy this book.
