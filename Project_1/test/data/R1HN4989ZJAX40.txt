
 
 
 This review is for the Criterion Collection DVD edition of the filmJules Dassin's "Night and the City" is the story of a swindler who attempts to make money through a wrestler but ends up getting a taste of his own medicine. The movie has some fine scenes of London and is very well written.The DVD has some fine special features which are as follows.Optional audio commentary by Glenn Erickson, Two interviews with director Jules Dassin,one new and the other from 1972, a comparison of the two film scores used for the Americna and British versions of the film, and a theatrical trailer.This is a great DVD to get.
