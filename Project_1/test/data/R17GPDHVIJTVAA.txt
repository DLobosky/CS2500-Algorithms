

You've Been Sentenced is a super fun game to play! There is a deck of cards that contains funny words, famous names, places and wild cards. All you have to do it create a grammatically correct sentence. Then each players read their sentence out loud to the other players. Then, the players have to decide whether or not the sentence is correct, makes sense and if points should be awarded. There are several add-on decks to make the game even more fun!
