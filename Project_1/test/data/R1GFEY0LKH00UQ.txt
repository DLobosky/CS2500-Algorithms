

The coasters designed by Royce Leather sold by Creative Leather Concepts have a nice weight to them and will not  stick to the bottoms of glasses like other coasters. The set contains 6 coasters that snuggle comfortably into a matching ,leather caddy. The coasters have non skid backing so they won't scratch your table. The set makes a fantastic addition to a home bar, coffee table, or outdoor patio. I use them on my glass coffee table. I love that when I pick up my glass of wine the coaster doesn't come with the glass.
