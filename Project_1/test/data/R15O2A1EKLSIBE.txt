

Though this is a combo pack with Blu-Ray and Standard DVD it will really only appeal to Blu-ray player owners for the higher def - which as noted by another reviewer does show some scratches - and the "exclusive" game on the Blu-ray.The Bonus features are the same as on the previous DVD edition except for the new "commercials" or "sneak peeks". But this is a very popular film and it's great that families with BD players can enjoy it in high def.
